About

Hello! I'm Farhan. I was born in Zanzibar, a vibrant island off the coast of Tanzania. I grew up surrounded by the ocean and rich Swahili culture. My mother is a skilled storyteller who passed down tales of ancient heroes and spirits, while my father works as a fisherman, teaching me the ways of the sea.

In my free time, I enjoy exploring the island's beautiful beaches, playing football with my friends, and learning about technology. I'm passionate about coding and dream of becoming a software engineer one day.

I'm also interested in environmental conservation and hope to use my skills to make a positive impact on my community.
